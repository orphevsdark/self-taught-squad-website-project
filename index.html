A LITTLE ABOUT US

You may wonder how a nerdy East Indian boy born and raised in Southern California met a wonderful Italian/English girl from Illinois. The oddest thing about about that story is that Victoria and Vikram met through their common connections to the city of Bozeman, Montana. When Vikram was working in technology marketing in Bozeman, he was fortunate enough to make many great friends there, some of whom knew Victoria from the time when she lived in Bozeman while completing her Bachelor's degree at Montana State University. In 2014, when Victoria was passing through town to ready herself for a move away from Montana, a mutual friend of Victoria nad Vikram introduced the two and they very quickly found themselves strongly attracted to one another. After a whirlwind beginning to their relationship, Victoria and Vikram realized in the Fall of 2014 that they had found something special together and chose to build a long-distance relationship together despite Victoria's educational goals taking her away from Montana to pursue her Master's degree in a town in Idaho that required over seven hours to reach by car from Bozeman. Despite the distance, Victoria and Vikram realized that the love hey share is worth the effort required to keep it strong, Even if they are far apart. After experiencing an amazing series of ups and downs together, Vikram proposed to Victoria in the Summer of 2016. Now, after Victoria and Vikram have waited patiently for the timing to work out well, it turns out that 2020 will be the year for Victoria and Vikram to officially tie the knot.
